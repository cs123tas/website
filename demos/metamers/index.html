Metamers Demo

In this demo, we ask you to edit power spectra -- which may represent the power spectra of light sources. The idea of a metamer is that although two spectra might be totally different, they might produce the same response for an RGB camera sensor, or in our visual system (another type of light sensor).

In order to use the Metamers Demo, follow these steps:
  1. Sketch a power spectrum on the left hand graph by clicking and dragging
  2. Sketch a power spectrum on the right hand graph by clicking and dragging
  3. Try to match the RGB output of the second graph with the first
